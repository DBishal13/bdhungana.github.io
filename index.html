Graduate Research and Teaching Assistant

As a Research Assistant at Center for Water Resiliency and Risk Reduction (CWR3) with its misson to empower communities to address the challenges of flooding and sea-level rise. CWR3 achieve this by providing technical assistance, data resources, and policy support to develop watershed master plans, improve Community Rating System (CRS) scores, and lower flood insurance premiums. Currently in Phase 2 we extendsed our efforts statewide, creating watershed master plans for all Florida communities aiming to enhance their CRS standings and reduce flood insurance costs for homeowners. My role as a GIS Analyst, is to simulate flood using CASCADE 2001 based on different storm events along with sea level rise of upto 5 feet, mapping numerous GIS layers, writing reports and finding solutions to reduce the flood risk probability.
